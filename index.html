About me.

I'm Agnieszka. I have over two years of commercial experience as a Junior Front-End Developer. In AC Project I have broadened my knowledge of Javascript and its framework Ember.js, SASS and code testing. I also learned how to work in a team of several people focused on the delivery of one large project designed for the hotel industry. By working in Multi-Design Studio I got experience in HTML, SCSS, Photoshop and PHP. It was much smaller team and we worked on several smaller projects for individual customers based on CMS Wordpress.
